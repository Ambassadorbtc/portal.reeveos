Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Does microneedling hurt?
Most clients describe it as a mild tingling. We apply numbing cream 20 minutes before treatment. Discomfort is minimal and well worth the results.
How many treatments will I need?
Most clients see visible improvement after 3 treatments. For significant concerns like acne scarring, a course of 6 treatments over 18 weeks delivers the most dramatic results.
Is it safe for sensitive or rosacea-prone skin?
Yes — our lymphatic approach is specifically designed to calm reactive skin. Natalie assesses every client individually and tailors the treatment intensity to your skin type.
What's the downtime after treatment?
Mild redness for 24-48 hours, similar to mild sunburn. Most clients return to normal activities the next day. We provide aftercare guidance and a post-treatment recovery cream.
Can I spread the cost?
Yes. Klarna and Clearpay are available on all treatments and packages. You can split payments interest-free.
What if I'm pregnant or on medication?
Some treatments aren't suitable during the first 12 weeks of pregnancy or with certain medications. Our consultation form flags any contraindications before you book — so there are no surprises on the day.
